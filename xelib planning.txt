1. GetLoadOrder: gets a list of available plugins in the order of loadorder.txt.
2. LoadPlugins: loads a specified load order of plugins.
3. GetGlobal: returns the value of a global variable.  E.g. DataPath, ProgramPath, ScriptsPath, FileCount, etc.
